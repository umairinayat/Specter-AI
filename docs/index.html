Capabilities

Everything you need,
nothing they can detect.

Six core features designed for real-time assistance without detection.

Invisible Overlay

A transparent, always-on-top window that is completely invisible to Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, and all screen-sharing software. Uses native OS APIs for screen-capture exclusion on macOS and Windows.

macOS: type:panel Windows: ContentProtection

Screen Reading (OCR)

Captures your screen on demand and extracts text using Tesseract.js running in a dedicated worker thread. The AI sees what you see.

Live Transcription

Records meeting audio via Web Audio API and transcribes every 10 seconds using Whisper. Supports Groq (free), OpenAI, or any custom endpoint.

500+ AI Models

Powered by OpenRouter with access to GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, Llama, DeepSeek, and 500+ more. Pick the model that fits your speed, quality, and budget needs.

Playbooks

Upload meeting prep, job descriptions, or notes as context documents. Active playbooks are automatically injected into every AI prompt for relevant, tailored responses.

Privacy First

Everything runs locally. No telemetry, no analytics, no data collection. Only extracted text is sent to the AI API — and only when you trigger it. Raw audio and screenshots never leave your machine.

Workflow

Three steps to
invisible AI assistance.

01

Capture Context

Press a hotkey and Specter captures your screen via OCR and transcribes meeting audio in real time. It sees what you see and hears what you hear.

Ctrl+Shift+Enter — Screenshot + OCR
Ctrl+Shift+Space — Toggle audio transcription
02

AI Processes

Screen text, audio transcript, your question, and active playbook context are assembled into a prompt and streamed to your chosen AI model on OpenRouter.

Ctrl+Enter — Ask AI with full context
03

Invisible Response

The AI response streams into a translucent overlay that only you can see. It stays on top of all windows but is completely invisible to screen share software.

Ctrl+\ — Toggle overlay visibility
