Capabilities

Everything you need.
Nothing they detect.

Invisible Overlay

Transparent, always-on-top window that is completely invisible to Zoom, Meet, Teams, and all screen-sharing software. Uses native OS APIs — WDA_EXCLUDEFROMCAPTURE on Windows, type:panel on macOS.

macOS: type:panel Win: FFI WDA_EXCLUDEFROMCAPTURE

Screen Reading

On-demand OCR via Tesseract.js in a worker thread. Smart crop captures only the active window.

Meeting Transcription

Record meetings with one click. Audio transcribed via Groq Whisper (free) or OpenAI, then answered by your chosen model.

500+ AI Models

GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, Llama, DeepSeek — pick the model that fits your speed, quality, and budget.

Playbooks & PDFs

Upload prep notes, job descriptions, or PDFs as context. Automatically injected into every prompt.

Privacy First — Your Data Never Leaves

Everything runs locally. No telemetry, no analytics, no data collection. Only extracted text goes to the AI — and only when you trigger it. Raw audio and screenshots never leave your machine.
