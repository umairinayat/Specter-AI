CAPABILITIES

Everything you need.
Nothing they detect.

01

Invisible Overlay

Transparent, always-on-top window invisible to Zoom, Meet, Teams, and all screen-sharing software. Native OS APIs — WDA_EXCLUDEFROMCAPTURE on Windows, type:panel on macOS.

macOS: type:panel Win: FFI WDA_EXCLUDEFROMCAPTURE
02

Screen Reading

On-demand OCR via Tesseract.js in a worker thread. Smart crop captures only the active window.

03

Meeting Transcription

Record meetings with one click. Audio transcribed via Groq Whisper, then answered by your chosen model.

04

500+ AI Models

GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, Llama, DeepSeek — pick the model that fits your speed, quality, and budget via OpenRouter.

05

Playbooks & PDFs

Upload prep notes, job descriptions, or PDFs as context. Automatically injected into every prompt.

06

Privacy First

Everything runs locally. No telemetry, no analytics, no data collection. Only extracted text goes to the AI — and only when you trigger it. Raw audio and screenshots never leave your machine.

Screen → Local OCR → Text Only → Overlay
WORKFLOW

Three steps to
invisible assistance.

01

Capture Context

Press a hotkey — Specter captures your screen via OCR and records meeting audio. It sees what you see and hears what you hear.

Ctrl+Shift+Enter Screenshot + OCR
Ctrl+Shift+Space Toggle audio
02

AI Processes

Screen text, transcript, your question, and playbook context are assembled and streamed to your chosen AI model on OpenRouter.

Ctrl+Enter Ask AI with full context
03

Invisible Response

The response streams into a translucent overlay only you can see. Always on top, never in the screen share.

Ctrl+\ Toggle overlay
